Book Training Sessions

All workshop sessions are booked through the SISO system. Sessions are scheduled based on demand and instructor availability.

Booking Process

  1. Access SISO system with your student credentials
  2. Navigate to Fabrication Station workshops
  3. Select appropriate level (must complete levels sequentially)
  4. Book available session time
  5. Ensure you have required safety clothing
Access SISO Booking System

Before Booking

  • Complete previous levels before booking next level
  • Review mandatory safety requirements
  • Check training program details
